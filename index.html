General Information

SHARCNet's Summer School mission is to provide a context for HPC researchers to come together and learn about advanced research computing.

Who: The courses are aimed at graduate students and other researchers who use, or are considering using SHARCNet HPC resources for Advanced Research Computing. Some understanding of HPC and the tools being taught is expected.
